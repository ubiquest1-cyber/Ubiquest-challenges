Lost at Sea – Team-Building Challenge

Your team is stranded in the middle of the ocean after your boat begins to sink. You’ve salvaged a set of items and must work together to determine which are most crucial for survival. This activity strengthens communication, decision-making, and collaboration. Duration: ~20 minutes.

Materials Needed

  • List of 15 items
  • Ranking sheets or paper
  • Pens
  • Timer or stopwatch

Instructions

  1. Individual Ranking (5 min):
    • Distribute the list of 15 salvaged items to each participant.
    • Ask everyone to independently rank the items from 1 (most important) to 15 (least important).
    • Discussion is not allowed during this stage.
  2. Group Discussion (10 min):
    • Divide participants into small teams (3–5 people).
    • Each member shares their ranking and explains reasoning.
    • Discuss differences and agree on a team ranking.
  3. Team Reflection & Debrief (5 min):
    • Compare your team’s final ranking with expert ranking.
    • Discuss differences and reasoning.
    • Reflect on communication, prioritization, and collaboration.

Debrief Questions

  • What was the most challenging decision and why?
  • How did your team communicate to reach consensus?
  • What did you learn about prioritization and collaboration?
  • How could these insights be applied in real-life team situations?
