Mobile Escape Game – Spaceship Challenge

Your team is stranded on a spaceship in the middle of space! In this interactive adventure, you must make quick decisions, solve puzzles, and cooperate to repair your ship before an alien attack reaches you. Duration: ~25 minutes. This challenge strengthens teamwork, strategic thinking, and communication under pressure.

Materials Needed

  • 1 smartphone per team (2–4 members max)
  • Actionbound app installed (iOS / Android)
  • Access to Wi-Fi or mobile data
  • QR code for the challenge (replace with actual code)

Operation Instructions

  1. Form Teams: Organize teams of 2–4 members.
  2. Install & Launch Actionbound: Download the Actionbound app for iOS or Android.
  3. Scan QR Code: Use your smartphone to scan the QR code below to access the adventure.
  4. Choose Team Name: Enter your team name when prompted.
  5. Optional Challenge: Set a timer to 10 minutes and see if your team can complete the adventure faster.
  6. Follow Onscreen Instructions: Solve puzzles, make decisions together, and advance through the adventure until the mission is complete.

Debrief Guidelines

  • Discuss the key decisions your team made and the reasoning behind them.
  • Identify moments where communication succeeded or could have been improved.
  • Reflect on time management and strategic planning during the challenge.
  • Consider how roles and responsibilities were shared and how collaboration impacted outcomes.
  • Share feelings and surprises that occurred during the adventure.

Expert Tips

  • Pay attention to all clues provided on the screen; small details can be crucial.
  • Discuss each decision as a team before acting—avoid acting individually.
  • Keep track of time and pace yourselves to avoid rushing important tasks.
  • Assign roles: navigator, clue decoder, communicator to optimize teamwork.
  • Encourage every team member to contribute and share ideas openly.
